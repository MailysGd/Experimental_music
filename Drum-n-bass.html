Drum n Bass

Description

Drum and Bass (also written as 'Drum n Bass' is a genre of electronic dance music characterized by fast breakbeats with heavy bass and sub-bass lines, samples and synthetizers. It posseses many sub-genres such as Jungle, Darkcore and Liquid.

History

This genre was born in the early 1990's in the United Kingdom's famous raves. From the popular sounds of the Breakbeat hardcore, people started to add more basslines and breakbeats to their melodies. Later on, Drum N Bass became really popular thanks to the many artists that made it evolde with their own artistic directions.
