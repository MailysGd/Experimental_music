SynthWave

Description

Synthwave, also called Retrowave, is a musical and artistic genre that was born in the early 2010's. It is mainly inspired by the 80's musics, ful of synthetizers sounds.

History

Synthwave was born in the early 2010's and quikcly gained popularity thanks to the movie Drive where "Nightcall" by Kavinsky was featured. It made sythwave a bit popular and now this genre is slowly rising to the top style of a lot of people

Darksynth

Darksynth is the sythwave but without the heartwarming felling of Miami and its beaches, palm trees and purple sun. Darksynth evokes distopic world with war everywhere and a more futuristic/cyberpunk aesthetic
