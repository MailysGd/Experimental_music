Dubstep

Description

Dupstep is an electronic music genre. It is the most representative genre of EDM because of its popularity during the early 2010's. Bass is the key element to dubstep and serves as the defining feature of the subgenre's sound. The herky-jerky rhythmic element of dubstep takes its cues from related genres like drum 'n' bass, garage, and grime.

History

Dupstep comes from the work of some Drum n Bass artists that own a nighclub. It began in London is the 90's
