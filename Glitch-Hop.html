Glitch-Hop

Description

Glitch-hop is an electronical music genre derived from glitch and hip-hop musics. It's basically a sub-genre of hip-hop with the usage of futurisic sounds typical to the glitch genre, and IDM technics like cutting, skipping or chopping.

History

Glitch-Hop starts its journey in the 90's on the US west coast and since it continues to grow up
