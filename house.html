House

Description

House music is a pretty simple style characterized by what is called the "four on the floor". it is a expression that describes a music where the kick hits on the four times. Often, voice samples are used in House music and has a funk style bass.

History

House derived from funk and disco with the addition of electronic sounds and the "four on the floor". After the beginning house has evolved in a lot of subgenres beacause it is influenced by the styles that are the most popular at the time.

Jakin House

Jackin house takes its name from the dans with the same name, it is charcaterized by the heavy use of old sample (voices or not).

French House

French House (or French touch) has a more fresh touch to it, it feel smoother to listen, it is lighter than traditional house. Its name comes from the fact that the first and most of french house producer are french

Future House

Future house has metalic drops, synthesizer and a muted melody (that is not the main thing you hear)

Tropical House

Tropical House explains itself, it is made to be listen on the beach it is relaxing due to a lower bpm, melodies are gives only good vibes and of course the foor on the floor is present.
